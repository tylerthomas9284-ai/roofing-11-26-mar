Our Story

Roofing Excellence Since 2010

RoofEdge was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to transform the roofing industry through transparency, quality workmanship, and unwavering customer commitment.

What started as a small team of passionate craftsmen has grown into one of Texas's most trusted roofing companies. Today, we've completed over 2,000 projects across Austin, Dallas, Houston, and San Antonio, earning our reputation one roof at a time.

We believe in doing things right the first time. Every project receives the same meticulous attention to detail, whether it's a residential repair or a large-scale commercial installation. Our certified crews combine modern technology with time-tested techniques to deliver roofs that protect families and businesses for decades.
