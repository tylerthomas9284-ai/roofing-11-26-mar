Signs You Need a Roof Replacement

If you notice any of these warning signs, it may be time for a new roof.

Age Over 20 Years

Most asphalt shingle roofs last 20-25 years. If yours is older, replacement is more cost-effective than repairs.

Curling or Missing Shingles

Widespread curling, buckling, or missing shingles indicate your roof has reached the end of its lifespan.

Granule Loss

Heavy granule accumulation in gutters means shingles are deteriorating and losing protective coating.

Multiple Leaks

Multiple leak points suggest underlying structural issues that require full replacement.

Sagging Roof Deck

Visible sagging indicates moisture damage to the decking and requires immediate attention.

Daylight Through Attic

Light visible through roof boards means gaps and holes that compromise your home's protection.
